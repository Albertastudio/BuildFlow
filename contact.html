Common Questions

Is the strategy call really free?+
100%. No hidden fees, no credit card required, no obligation to purchase. It's a genuine 30-minute conversation where we learn about your business and tell you honestly what would help most.
Do you only work with Calgary contractors?+
Not at all — we're based in Calgary but serve contractors across all Canadian provinces. Everything is handled remotely, so location is never a barrier.
How long until I see results?+
Missed call text-back can be live within 24 hours — and you'll see an immediate impact on lead recovery. Full system results (more bookings, higher close rate) typically show within 30–60 days.
What if I'm not ready to buy — can I still call?+
Absolutely. We're happy to answer questions, give advice, or just point you in the right direction — even if you're not ready to move forward yet. No pressure, ever.
What information should I have ready for the call?+
Just a rough idea of: how leads currently come in, how many calls you get per week, your average job value, and your biggest frustration with your current setup. That's enough for us to work with.
Do you offer ongoing support after setup?+
Yes — all plans include ongoing support. We do monthly reviews, make updates, and are available directly whenever something needs fixing. We stay with you for as long as you need us.
