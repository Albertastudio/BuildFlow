How It Started

Born on a Job Site.
Built in Calgary.

It started with a simple observation. A roofing contractor in Calgary was losing three to four calls a day — not because he didn't want the work, but because he was on the roof when his phone rang. By the time he called back, the homeowner had already booked someone else.

We saw the same story repeated across dozens of contractors in Alberta. Talented, hardworking business owners — roofers, plumbers, electricians, HVAC techs — losing thousands of dollars every month not because of a lack of skill, but because of a broken system around how leads were captured and followed up.

So in 2022, five of us — a web designer, a developer, an AI engineer, an automation specialist, and a business strategist — came together in Calgary with one shared goal: build the system we wished these contractors already had.

Not a website. Not a chatbot. Not a CRM that takes six months to learn. A complete, connected revenue system — designed specifically for the way contractors run their businesses — that works automatically, from the first call to the five-star review.

That's BuildFlow. And we're just getting started.
