FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to sign a long-term contract? +
No. All monthly plans are month-to-month — you can cancel anytime without penalty. Annual plans are billed upfront and save you 20%.
How long does setup take? +
Missed call text-back and basic CRM can be live within 24 hours. A full system setup (website + CRM + AI agent + automations) typically takes 7–14 days depending on complexity.
Can I add services later? +
Absolutely. You can start with the Starter plan or just a website and add AI services, automation, or add-ons at any time. Everything is modular and connects together.
Do you work with contractors outside Alberta? +
Yes — we work with contractors across Canada. Our systems are built for any province or territory. We also serve US-based contractors on request.
What's included in the free strategy call? +
A 30-minute call where we audit your current lead flow, identify where you're losing money, and walk you through which BuildFlow services would have the biggest impact for your specific business — no pressure, no pitch deck.
Is the website one-time or recurring? +
The website build is a one-time fee. Hosting is a separate monthly subscription (starting at $20/month) which covers SSL, backups, and server maintenance.
